WHAT IS CHESS

Chess is one of the oldest and most popular board games. It is played by two opponents on a checkered board with specially designed pieces of contrasting colours, commonly white and black. The objective of the game is to capture the opponent's king.

How the Chess Pieces Move
  1. The King moves from its square to a neighboring square,
  2. the Rook can move in its line or row,
  3. the Bishop moves diagonally,
  4. the Queen may move like a Rook or a Bishop,
  5. the Knight jumps in making the shortest move that is not a straight one, and.
  6. the Pawn moves one square straight ahead.
